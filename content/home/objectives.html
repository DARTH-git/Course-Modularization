This workshop will offer personalized teaching and guidance to participants. During the workshop, the instructors will help participants on a case by case basis to:

  1. Construct a decision model based on their existing research problem

  2. Move to the next stage(s) of their model developing process

  3. Transform their models in a different software into R

  4. Understand feedback of their models given by the instructors

  5. Conduct specific analyses in R
